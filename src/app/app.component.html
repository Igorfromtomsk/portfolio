Interactive board

making of hexagonal grid for some stuff

How does it works

hexagonal grid

I made grid by rounds from center. At first I calculate how much rounds I need for filling all element, then I make every round with translation and rotation from center.

When I make third and subsequent rounds, I am looking for vector between two basic cells — that is how I can find centers of other cells at round. And I need to check if the new cell in the element before drawing.

terminal
